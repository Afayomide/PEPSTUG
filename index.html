ABOUT US

PEPSTUG INTERNATIONAL CONSULT (PIC) is a commonwealth accredited educational organization committed to train/retrain teachers, students and corporate educational groups on modern effective approaches to ensure performance and enhance quality service delivery.

PEPSTUG is a 3-1 learning aid/game that simplifies and innovatively combines spellings, transcription and the sounds of the English language words to aid students’ consistent practice and performance in both written (theory) and oral (spoken) text/exams. PEPSTUG which has been patented, arouses students’ interest to learn as it enhances practice and as well solves the common monotonous way of learning is teacher class-based.

Our Activities
  • Training of teachers and students on the effective use of PEPSTUG to aid quality service delivery and performance.
  • Retraining English/Phonics teachers on the effective materials, methods and approaches of teaching to ensure performance (Free speech workshop for teachers every session)
  • Commonwealth Proficiency Assessment Test and Award for certificate classes. There will be a special revision training by PEPSTUG Chief Trainer before the certificate student assessment test. The assessment test aims to evaluate students’ preparation and to ascertain their ability for international exams/competitions and scholarship opportunities. Registered students for this special training and test will be given the commonwealth English proficiency performance statement and best among them will be given an award. Thus, this assessment and training could as well serve as a means to examine the students’ ability in preparation towards their respective national examinations (WAEC & NECO) both at the junior and the senior school levels.
